Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

    Male:

  • Sunday: Kwasi 0
  • Monday: Kwadwo 1
  • Tuesday: Kwabena 2
  • Wednesday: Kwaku 3
  • Thursday: Yaw 4
  • Friday: Kofi 5
  • Saturday: Kwame 6

    Female:

  • Sunday: Akosua 0
  • Monday: Adwoa 1
  • Tuesday: Abenaa 2
  • Wednesday: Akua 3
  • Thursday: Yaa 4
  • Friday: Afua 5
  • Saturday: Ama 6
